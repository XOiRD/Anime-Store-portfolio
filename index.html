UAE ANIME STORE

About Us

With a smile, UAE Anime store sell products and services related to Anime. In 2018, UAE Anime store was founded in Umm AL Quwain, U.A.E. to reach out and connect the anime community locally and worldwide. As part of our outreach, we help in making workshops, manage activities, and sponsor events. We encourage and support youth talent to grow and show their potential.

Our Services

Selling Anime Products

For anime fans, we sell many anime products like  anime mystery boxes, anime swords, posters, keychains and more. 

Organizing Events

We are making events or having partnership to help the organizers in preparing the events. 

Sponsoring Events

Sponsoring in terms of gifts, competitions, workshops, stage performance and more.

Workshops

We can provide workshops for different ages and topics such as game development and drawing.

Supporting Businesses

Helping people to start their businesses, develop current businesses and provide opportunities to them.

Websites & Game Dev.

We are making websites for any business, experience in game development and designing.

Supplier

Supplying anime products in wholesale prices for large quantities orders. 

Artist Team

We have an artist team that can provide live drawings, wall paintings and Manga drawing.
